Competition Project

Music Gloves (National Undergraduate IoT Design Competition 2014) [Android, C] | 2014.7 ~ 2014.8

  • Android Part Source Code
  • News Report 1, News Report 2
  • Wearable portable entertainment equipment
  • This is a pair of gloves with pressure sensors. Sensors are connected with a SCM. Pressure data are collected and sent to one Android device through WiFi.
    The Android App will get pressure data and simulate the sound of guitar, drum and piano.User can switch instrument.
    You are able to save your performance and upload it to the cloud and download it into another Android device.
    You can also cooperate with your friends to play the same song using ONLY ONE phone and a number of gloves like playing a duet.
  • Won National Undergraduate IoT Design Competition 2014 Finals First Prize & Wi-Fi Special Award.
  • Won Shanghai Jiao Tong University Hackthon 2014 Best Creative Award &The Most Popular Works.

Research Projects

Particle Simulation about Reclaiming on Sand Piles using CUDA [C++, CUDA] | 2015.6 ~ 2015.9

  • Particle simulation based on GPU parallel computing,inspired by this paper(Here)
  • Reach nearly real-time rendering with the help of CUDA
  • Use the DEM(Discrete Element Method) to simulate particles on the surface
    Internal particles are computed by the BCRE method.
    Some modifications were made in order to apply this method to CUDA.
  • The simulation speed increased by 140 times compared with the original one implemented in the paper.
  • An industrial scene are built to display our performance.

Distributed Information Collection System [Android, Arduino] | 2013.9 ~ 2014.9

  • Distributed information collection in a farmland
  • Sensors in SMCs collect a variety kind of data
    . SMCs and the Android device are connected either by Wi-Fi or Bluetooth 4.0.open Wi-Fi AP or Bluetooth 4.0.
  • The Android device can display the current environment in the farmland, including temperature, humidity and light intensity.
    If there are some condition which may impair the crops, they will be recorded.
